# Car-sales-SQL-project
A beginner-friendly SQL project focused on analyzing car sales data to gain practical experience in querying, aggregating, and extracting business insights from a relational database. This project explores sales performance, customer behavior, and regional revenue trends using SQL.


##  Project Objectives

- Practice writing clean, efficient SQL queries.
- Extract meaningful business insights from structured data.
- Understand customer behaviors and product performance.
- Learn how to perform joins, groupings, filtering, and aggregations in SQL.

- ## Key Questions Answered

1. How many car brands are available?
2. How many customers are in the database?
3. What is the total revenue and number of cars sold?
4. Which car brands perform the best in terms of sales and quantity sold?
5. Which cities have generated over $2 million in revenue?
6. What is the most popular car model?
7. Who are the top 10 customers by number of orders?
8. Which customers have never placed an order?
9. 9.	categorize orders based on quantity sold; 'High' if quantity_sold > 5, else 'Low'
10.	Get total sales per customer, replacing missing sales amount with 0
11.	Which car brands are popular among customers between age 25 and 30
12.	What is the performance of different car colors in terms of sales quantity?
13.	Which month made the highest and lowest revenue?

##  Technologies Used

- SQL (PostgreSQL)
- SQL Joins, Aggregations, Group By, Having, Sorting
